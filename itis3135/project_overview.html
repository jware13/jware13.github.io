Client Project

Project Name: Seagull Sustainability

Project Overview

The Seagull Sustainability project is a web application designed to promote sustainable practices and raise awareness about environmental issues. The application will provide users with information on how to reduce their carbon footprint, tips for sustainable living, and a platform for sharing ideas and resources.

Seagull Sustainability's website serves as a way to introduce and promote cost-effective, eco-friendly alternative to plastics.

The intended users of this website include:

  • Environmental Organizations
  • Businesses looking for sustainable materials
  • Environmentally conscious customers
  • Potential investors/partners
