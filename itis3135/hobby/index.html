Why Reading Matters to Me

Reading is a way for me to connect with my imagination, explore new ideas, and understand the world around me. It's a way for me to feel less stressed and more relaxed, and it's a way for me to enjoy a good story. I've always liked how I can imagine myself in the world that the author creates for me and can see my self following the plot that they set.
