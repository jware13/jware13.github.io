About Us

At The Jolly Wallaby's Plushies, we believe everyone deserves a soft, comforting companion to brighten up their day. Inspired by the playful and friendly nature of wallabies and many other amazing creatures of Australia.

We wanted to create a plushie that can serve as a source of comfort to our customers and that is why they come as regular soft plushie and weighted plushies so you can choose to match your comfort style!
